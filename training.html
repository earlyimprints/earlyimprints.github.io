Pre and Perinatal Training

with Charisse Basquin and Mary Jackson

Learn the latest in Pre and Perinatal Therapy. Through experiential work you will explore your own developmental history and early imprints. Discover how to attune to babies and clients' early life material to enhance therapeutic outcomes.

For:

  • Anyone working with babies, children and families.
  • Therapists and professionals working with adults interested in early trauma and birth.

Pre and Perinatal Training

This training prepares participants to help their clients with pre-verbal imprinting. We will teach how to support clients in developing the skills to self regulate and differentiate past trauma from present-time triggers. In this way they can make their implicit memories explicit transforming chronic patterns into healthier behaviors.

Learning is facilitated through experiential exercises where the theory is integrated and put to work in a group setting designed to produce a safe and often fun space.

Lectures provide a sound framework of understanding, and footage of family sessions show examples of how to apply the material in your practice.
